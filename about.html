ANIME TALK

Talk with your favorite anime character!

Anime Talk is an interactive chatting web app that allows you to have a conversation with your favorite character with the power of AI. The app uses deep learning to generate deep fakes from pictures generated by AI. Sound files collected for each anime character are used to synthesize lip synced videos that combine the deep fakes and the sound database.
